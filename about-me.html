Family

Throughout my life, I have been truly blessed with a family that has supported me and nurtured every hobby and passion I’ve come to love. I was fortunate to have not just one, but two sets of parents who made sure I was cared for and able to fully enjoy my childhood. Today, I am even more blessed to share my life with my amazing wife, Alicia, and our four beloved fur-babies. I look forward with excitement and gratitude as our little family continues to grow, and I can't wait to pass on the same love, support, and blessings that I was so lucky to receive.

Golf

Since the age of seven, I have been immersed in the game of golf — a sport that challenges both the body and the mind, requiring physical skill, mental toughness, and quick problem-solving. Over the years, playing on countless courses has allowed me to experience new places, meet wonderful people, and create lasting memories. Golf is a beautiful game that no one can ever truly master, yet we continue to chase perfection from childhood all the way through our golden years — and that journey is part of what makes it so special to me.

Games

Growing up, video games were my gateway into the world of technology and all its little quirks. Whether it was mastering the latest game, troubleshooting when things inevitably went wrong, or just wondering how it all worked behind the scenes, I was hooked. Fast forward to today — and not much has changed! I still love diving into video games and seeing how far they’ve come. From solo adventures to chaotic online battle royales and sprawling MMORPGs, the possibilities are endless — and I’m always ready to jump in and try them all!

Technology

My passion for technology has been a lifelong pursuit, steadily growing over the years. From gaming and building my own computer from the ground up to troubleshooting both hardware and software issues, I have continually sought to deepen my understanding. Now, as I dive into the world of coding, I am energized by the endless possibilities it offers. Technology is an ever-expanding ocean of opportunity, and I am committed to exploring it for years to come.
