Getting to know your Coder

Created by Colby Taylor on April 28th, 2025

The Spark

From an early age, I developed a strong passion for technology, inspired by my first experiences with devices such as the Game Boy, early cell phones, and, most notably, computers and the internet. This curiosity drove me to continuously seek a deeper understanding of the field. In 2023, I began working hands-on with hardware, which further fueled my interest in the broader scope of technology development. Motivated to expand my skills, I started learning to code in early 2025, embarking on a rewarding journey of professional growth. I am eager to continue building my expertise and excited for the opportunities that lie ahead.

The Beginning

I began taking courses through Udemy and collaborating with fellow developers to build a strong foundation of knowledge, with a focus on understanding not just how to code, but why certain approaches and best practices are important. My goal has always been to make programming an integral part of who I am, rather than simply a means to a career or source of income. Since then, I have become proficient in a variety of programming languages, including:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • React

The Road to the Future

With these tools, I was able to look beyond the physical product and appreciate the underlying structure and intricacies that bring web pages to life. I recognized the attention to detail, the dedication, and the passion embedded within each creation. It became clear to me that this was something I wanted to contribute to — to help craft works that are not only functional but enduring, inspiring me to continually grow and refine my skills.

With the support of my wife and family, I had a strong foundation to help me navigate the challenges and opportunities along this journey. Each experience — both positive and negative — played a crucial role in shaping who I am today and continues to guide me toward the future I am working to build.

I am enthusiastic about the future and committed to continuous learning and growth. With dedication and passion, I look forward to seeing where this journey will lead and the opportunities it will bring. ♥
